A Short Biography

I may not have been around the world, but ive certainly been around the block. ive worked in the hospitality industry for the last 10 years. I also work for a design firm and helped produce million dollar weddings and parties.

Education

After completing high school in Indianapolis, I attended Holy Cross College in South Bend, IN. The easiest way to help people remember Holy Cross is to remind them that it is the school Rudy attended in the movie "Rudy" before he was accepted into Notre Dame. After completing my Associates degree there I moved to Louisville, KY where I recieved an associates degree in Culinary arts and a Bachelors Degree in Hospitality Management.

Work and Travels

I came back to Indianapolis after I finished all my schooling and started work at my Dad's law firm; it was work unrelated to my degrees but he needed help running his private firm and I was happy to lend a hand doing all the small and menial things while he focused on more important and complex problems. When he could get along without me, I moved to Colorado to work in my field. I cooked at a couple restaurants in Aspen and Snowmass. I also worked for a wedding and event planner in Aspen; we did some amazing thing there, things that I will always be proud of.I loved living in the Colorado Rockies, but being that far from my family in Indianapolis was difficult. Eventually I decided to move back. Now i'm starting a new chapter in my life at Kenzie Acadamey.
